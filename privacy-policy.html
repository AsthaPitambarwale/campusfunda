Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information at Campus Funda.

Last Updated: January 1, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Campus Funda ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website campusfunda.com and use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Personal identification information (name, email address, phone number)
  • Academic information (university, course, year of study)
  • Payment information processed through secure third-party gateways
  • Profile data and preferences set within the platform
  • Device and usage data including IP address, browser type, and pages visited

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Campus Funda platform
  • Process transactions and send related information including purchase confirmations
  • Send administrative information such as updates and security alerts
  • Personalize your experience and deliver content relevant to your course
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our platform and services
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and customer service requests

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personally identifiable information to others. We may share your data with:

  • Service providers who assist in operating our platform (payment processors, analytics providers)
  • Publishers and authors to fulfill your digital content orders
  • Law enforcement or regulatory bodies when required by law

All third-party service providers are contractually obligated to keep your information confidential.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information. This includes SSL encryption for all data transmitted on our platform, secure data storage, and restricted access controls. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

6. Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our platform and hold certain information to enhance your experience. You can control the use of cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some features of the platform.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Object to or restrict the processing of your data
  • Request data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at seema@campusfunda.com .

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
