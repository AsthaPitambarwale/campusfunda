Our Mission

Making Quality Education Accessible to Every Student

Campus Funda has been focused on making learning easy, advanced & accessible for over a decade. We accomplish this by continuous innovation and research towards understanding how learning happens. From eBooks and video lectures to MCQ tests and discussion forums, everything you need to score better is right here.

Syllabus-specific eBooks from top publishers like Techknowledge Publication, Idol Publication, and more — available for Pune University, Mumbai University, GTU, MSBTE, and DBATU.

✍

Our Journey

2012

Founded in Pune

Campus Funda launched with a vision to digitize study materials for engineering students in Maharashtra.

2015

MCQ Platform Launched

Introduced an interactive MCQ test platform covering all major engineering subjects and universities.

2018

Mobile App Released

Launched the Campus Funda mobile app on Google Play, reaching 100,000+ downloads in the first year.

2022

500,000 Students Milestone

Crossed half a million registered students across India with 100,000+ study resources available on the platform.
